Got Questions? We've Got Answers!

What time is the worship service?

Our worship service starts at 10:00 AM and ends at 12:30 PM every Sunday.

When is the youth meeting?

Join us on the 3rd Sunday of every month from 12:00 PM to 1:00 PM for an exciting youth meeting!

What time is the ladies ministry?

Ladies ministry takes place from 7:00 PM to 8:00 PM. Come and be a part of this empowering group!

Is there a fasting prayer session?

Yes, we have fasting prayer every Friday from 11:00 AM to 1:00 PM. Join us in seeking spiritual growth and renewal!

When is the prayer & Bible study?

Join us for prayer & Bible study every Saturday from 7:30 PM to 9:00 PM. Dive deep into the Word with us!
